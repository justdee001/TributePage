Robert John Downey Jr. is an American actor and singer.
  • Stupendous acting skills and delicate charm are some of the few traits to express the Ironman, “Robert Downey Jr.”. Debuting at the age of 5 in his father’s film ‘Pound’, Downey Jr got an early exposure to the world of cinemas and glamour alike.
  • Critical and commercial success at a young age wasn’t something surprising for Robert Downey Jr as he received his first Academy Award nomination for playing the title role in the movie ‘Chaplin’.
  • Bearing the brunt of losing some of the best years of his life due to his excessive obsession towards drugs, Robert Downey Jr came back sober and went on to become the highest paid Hollywood actor in three subsequent years from 2012 to 2015 as per the Forbes magazine.
  • With a life as unbelievable and glamorous as a classic Hollywood drama, Robert Downey Jr continues to win over movie lover around the world with his energetic persona and convincing acting performances.
